{nm}=∑i=0m(mi)(−1)m−iinm!=∑i=0mm!i!(m−i)!m!(−1)m−iin=∑i=0min(−1)m−ii!(m−i)!=∑i=0mini!×(−1)m−i(m−i)!\begin{aligned}{n\brace m} &= \sum_{i=0}^{m}\frac{\tbinom{m}{i}(-1)^{m-i}i^n}{m!}\\&=\sum_{i=0}^{m}\dfrac{m!}{i!(m-i)!m!}(-1)^{m-i}i^n\\&=\sum_{i=0}^{m}\dfrac{i^n(-1)^{m-i}}{i!(m-i)!}\\&=\sum_{i=0}^{m}\dfrac{i^n}{i!}\times\dfrac{(-1)^{m-i}}{(m-i)!}\end{aligned}{mn​}​=i=0∑m​m!(im​)(−1)m−iin​=i=0∑m​i!(m−i)!m!m!​(−1)m−iin=i=0∑m​i!(m−i)!in(−1)m−i​=i=0∑m​i!in​×(m−i)!(−1)m−i​​
